About Me

Hi! I'm Leura (or Cue on the internet), a 20 year digital artist and currently a student. My interests include: Illustration and design, animation, cute clothes, literary tropes, video games as an art form, fandom as methodology and I have a newfound interest in coding.

When I'm not drawing, you might find me doing side quests, playing games, attempt to code or taking a nap. I admire curiosity, and kindess in others- people who care deeply, whether about ideas, art, or the world around them. What matters most to me isn’t that we agree on everything, but that we’re both willing to learn, share passions, and put genuine effort into the things (and people) we love.
