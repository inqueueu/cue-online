About Me

Hi! I'm Leura (or Cue on the internet), a 20 year digital artist and currently a student. My interests include: Illustration and design, animation, cute clothes, literary tropes, video games as an art form, fandom as methodology and I have a newfound interest in coding.

When I'm not drawing, you might find me doing side quests, playing games, attempt to code or taking a nap. I admire curiosity, and kindess in others- people who care deeply, whether about ideas, art, or the world around them.

Project Documentation


I quickly realised just how much content I can add onto this website, If i choose to, so I decided to keep it simple and not add too much. I have unintentionally created a personal website that can be used to display my work too. I didn't think I'd end up with a safe space on the internet, but here we are. I am not actually expecting anyone to go through all of this, because it's so personal I can compare it to the same as getting to know someone. Everyone is different, and our curiosities towards people are different too. If there's something here that can resonate with you, then i am glad.
